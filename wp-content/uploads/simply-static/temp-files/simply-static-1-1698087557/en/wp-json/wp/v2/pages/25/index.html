Our company, Elltrans, is a team of experienced drivers and forwarders who are distinguished by high professional qualifications and strong personality traits. Their dedication, sense of responsibility, and reliability in carrying out assigned duties are key qualities that have contributed to the dynamic development of our company.<\/p>

History and Experience:
The Elltrans company is based in Gda\u0144sk and has been active in the transportation and forwarding industry since 1998. Our established position in the market is a result of our consistent adherence to regulations and standards. We take pride in having the necessary documents to conduct our operations, including liability insurance for Road Transport in both Domestic and International sectors, as well as Forwarder's Liability Insurance, and liability insurance for the transportation of goods within Germany.<\/p>

Our Mission:
Our priority is to provide our customers with the highest standard of services. That's why we have implemented a quality management system compliant with the requirements of the PN-EN ISO 9001:2009 standard, and we are currently working on implementing HACCP. Our road transportation services cover both Poland and the countries of Western and Central Europe.<\/p>

We invite you to learn more about our company and our offerings on our website. We are ready to meet your expectations and provide professional services in the field of transportation and forwarding.<\/p><\/div><\/div>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Technical infrastructure<\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Our modern vehicle fleet is a true pride of our company. We have as many as 90 specialized vehicles that are ready to meet even the most demanding transportation needs. Our fleet consists of various types of vehicles, including 22 three-axle tractor units and trailers such as refrigerated, side-opening refrigerated, and curtainsiders. This means that we can handle various types of cargo and provide the appropriate temperature and transport conditions for each product.<\/p>

Our fleet is constantly being updated because we understand the importance of maintaining the highest standards in the transportation industry. All of our vehicles are equipped with state-of-the-art GPS systems, which means that we can always track the location and route of our vehicles, ensuring complete control and safety for our customers.<\/p>

Moreover, the majority of our vehicles comply with the latest emission standards \"Euro 5\" and \"Euro 6,\" demonstrating our commitment to environmental protection and providing eco-friendly transportation solutions.<\/p>

Trust us to deliver your cargo safely, on time, and with attention to detail. Our modern vehicle fleet is a guarantee that your transportation needs will be met at the highest level.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
